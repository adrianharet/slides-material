    The number of infected at time \(t+1\) is given by the recursion:
    \begin{flalign*}
        \qquad I_{t+1} &= I_t + \alpha (N - I_t), &&
    \end{flalign*}

    or, written as a \emph{difference equation}: 
    \begin{flalign*}
        \qquad \Delta I & = I_{t+1} - I_t &&\\ 
                 & = \alpha (N - I_t). &&
    \end{flalign*}
